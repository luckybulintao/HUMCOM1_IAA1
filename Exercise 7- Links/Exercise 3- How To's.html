How to Fix Bad Sleeping Habits

Step 1: Evaluate your current situation

  1. What are the reasons for your bad sleeping habits?

    2. Step 2: Create a plan of action

    1. Set your sleeping time
    2. Stop doing activities during the night

      3. Step 3: Develop good time management skills

      1. Do things during the day
      2. Plan your activities
      3. Avoid piling up work

        4. Step 4: Start slowly

        1. You can sleep atleast 30 minutes earlier than your usual sleeping time and get used to it
        2. Increase the time until you reached your desired regular sleeping time
        3. Get used to your set time before increasing the time

          4. Step 5: Stick to the plan

          1. It takes 21 days to build a habit
          2. Your body will adapt to changes if you do it consistently
